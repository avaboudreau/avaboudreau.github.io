My partner for the project, Allison Vomaska, and I created the game "Mad-Fish" in our CSC106 class. The purpose of the game is for the player to eat as many pink fish as they can without getting eaten by the shark. To play, one must simply move using the arrow keys. If the player goes over a pink fish, the score increases by one point. If the player goes over the shark, then it is game over!
